The Rolex Daytona is a high-performance chronograph designed for professional racing drivers. Introduced in 1963, it has become a timeless classic. The Daytona is equipped with a tachymetric scale, three counters, and pushers, allowing drivers to measure elapsed time and calculate average speed.
